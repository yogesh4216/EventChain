Step into a magical evening at the “Moonlight Masala Fest” — a fictional celebration where music, dance, and laughter light up the night. Set under glowing lanterns, this event brings together energetic Garba moves, delicious street food, and joyful vibes. Friends gather, stories flow, and every beat invites you to dance. It’s not just an event, it’s a memory in the making — filled with colors, culture, and unstoppable fun.
